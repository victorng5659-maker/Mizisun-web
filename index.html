COMPANY PROFILE
Mizisun Doors and Windows, founded in 1998, is a modern home door and window enterprise that integrates design, R&D, manufacturing and sales, providing one-stop customized services. Our supply chain covers system windows, doors, entrance doors, shower rooms, and outdoor systems.

Headquartered in Foshan, Guangdong — the famous door and window manufacturing base, we have a 150,000 m² intelligent manufacturing base, with advanced automation, full welding, nano-coating and CNC technology. We focus on precision, efficiency, and stable delivery, committed to creating quiet, energy-saving, and beautiful homes.

In 2025, Mizisun officially entered the Malaysia market, with its first showroom in Johor Bahru. We look forward to creating quality homes for Malaysian families and partners.

美之选门窗（Mizisun Doors and Windows）成立于1998年，是一家集设计、研发、制造与销售为一体的现代化系统门窗企业，为客户提供一站式量身定制服务。我们拥有完整的垂直供应链，涵盖系统窗、系统门、入户门、淋浴房与户外系统等系列。

我们总部位于中国广东佛山——著名的门窗制造基地，拥有15万平方米智能制造基地，并引入先进自动化设备及全焊接系统、纳米喷涂工艺与CNC技术。我们以高精度、高效率与稳定交付为核心，致力于为全球家庭打造安静、节能与美观的居家体验。
